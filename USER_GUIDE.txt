3. CORE FEATURES
==================================================

3.1 SUPPLY-DEMAND FORECASTING
------------------------------
Location: Main Navigation > Supply & Demand

PURPOSE:
Predict future tutor supply and student demand to optimize staffing.

HOW TO USE:
1. Select forecast date range using date picker
2. Choose quick presets: "Next Month", "Next Quarter", "Next 6 Months"
3. Click "Generate Forecast"
4. Review prediction chart with confidence intervals
5. Export data as CSV or Excel for planning

INTERPRETING RESULTS:
- Green line: Tutor supply
- Blue line: Student demand
- Red shaded area: Supply gap (demand > supply)
- Confidence interval: Gray shaded region
- Higher confidence = more reliable prediction

ACTION ITEMS:
- Supply gaps: Recruit more tutors
- Excess supply: Reduce recruiting, focus on demand
- Seasonal patterns: Plan ahead for busy periods

3.2 CHURN PREDICTION
--------------------
Location: Main Navigation > Churn Prediction

PURPOSE:
Identify customers at risk of leaving and prevent churn.

HOW TO USE:
1. Review list of at-risk customers
2. Filter by risk level: High, Medium, Low
3. Click customer name to view detailed risk factors
4. Review recommended actions for each customer
5. Export list to share with customer success team

RISK INDICATORS:
- High Risk (red): Immediate action needed
- Medium Risk (yellow): Monitor closely
- Low Risk (green): Standard engagement

COMMON RISK FACTORS:
- Declining usage: Sessions decreased by >50%
- Low satisfaction: Ratings below 3.0
- Payment issues: Failed payments or late payments
- Support tickets: Multiple unresolved issues
- Engagement drop: Not logging in for 30+ days

RECOMMENDED ACTIONS:
- Personal outreach: Call or email customer
- Discount offer: Provide limited-time incentive
- Product demo: Re-engage with features they're not using
- Feedback survey: Understand their concerns
- Upgrade path: Offer premium features trial

3.3 CAMPAIGN RECOMMENDATIONS
----------------------------
Location: Main Navigation > Campaign Recommendations

PURPOSE:
AI-powered suggestions for customer engagement campaigns.

HOW TO USE:
1. Browse recommended campaigns
2. Review target segment and estimated ROI
3. Click campaign card for detailed strategy
4. Filter by campaign type: Acquisition, Retention, Re-engagement
5. Export campaign plan as PDF for team review

CAMPAIGN TYPES:
- Acquisition: Attract new customers
- Retention: Keep current customers engaged
- Re-engagement: Win back inactive customers
- Upsell: Encourage upgrades to premium tiers
- Referral: Incentivize customer referrals

CAMPAIGN DETAILS:
- Target segment: Who to target
- Channel: Email, SMS, in-app, push notification
- Timing: Best time to launch
- Message: Suggested copy and CTA
- Budget: Estimated cost
- ROI: Expected return on investment

3.4 TUTOR PERFORMANCE
---------------------
Location: Main Navigation > Tutor Performance

PURPOSE:
Monitor and rank tutor performance across key metrics.

HOW TO USE:
1. View tutor leaderboard sorted by performance score
2. Filter by performance level: Top Performers, Average, Needs Support
3. Click tutor name to view detailed profile
4. Sort by different metrics: Sessions, Rating, Revenue
5. Export performance report for reviews

PERFORMANCE METRICS:
- Performance Score: Composite score (0-100)
- Total Sessions: Number of sessions completed
- Average Rating: Student ratings (1-5 stars)
- Student Satisfaction: Percentage of satisfied students
- Revenue Generated: Total revenue from sessions
- Trend: Improving, stable, or declining

ACTION ITEMS:
- Top Performers: Recognize and reward excellence
- Average: Provide standard support and training
- Needs Support: Offer additional training and mentorship

4. ANALYTICS TOOLS
==================================================

4.1 SESSION VELOCITY MONITORING
--------------------------------
Location: Main Navigation > Velocity Monitoring

PURPOSE:
Track session creation rate in real-time to detect anomalies.

HOW TO USE:
1. View current velocity chart (sessions per hour)
2. Compare to baseline average velocity
3. Monitor for anomaly alerts (yellow/red indicators)
4. Adjust time window: 1h, 6h, 24h, 7d
5. Investigate spikes or drops in real-time

INTERPRETING VELOCITY:
- Normal: Within expected range (green)
- Elevated: Above normal but acceptable (yellow)
- Anomaly: Significant deviation requiring investigation (red)

COMMON CAUSES OF ANOMALIES:
- Marketing campaign launch (positive spike)
- System outage (drop to zero)
- Seasonal events (holidays, back-to-school)
- Competitor actions (sudden drop)
- Viral social media post (positive spike)

4.2 ANOMALY DETECTION
----------------------
Location: Integrated across all features

PURPOSE:
Automatically detect unusual patterns across metrics.

HOW IT WORKS:
- Machine learning models analyze historical data
- Compares current values to expected ranges
- Triggers alerts when anomalies detected
- Severity scored: Low, Medium, High, Critical

WHAT TO DO WHEN ANOMALY DETECTED:
1. Review alert details and severity
2. Check related metrics for context
3. Investigate root cause (system, market, operations)
4. Take corrective action if needed
5. Document incident for future reference

4.3 SUCCESS TRACKING
--------------------
Location: Main Navigation > Success Tracking

PURPOSE:
Monitor first session success rates for new customers.

KEY METRICS:
- First Session Success Rate: % of successful first sessions
- Average Time to First Session: Days from signup to first session
- First Session Quality: Rating and feedback
- Conversion Rate: % who book second session

IMPROVING SUCCESS RATES:
- Onboarding optimization: Improve new user experience
- Tutor matching: Better algorithmic matching
- Expectations setting: Clearer communication pre-session
- Technical support: Reduce technical issues

5. ALERTS & MONITORING
==================================================

5.1 UNDERSTANDING ALERTS
------------------------
Alert Icon: Bell icon in top-right corner
- Red badge: Number of unread alerts
- Pulsing animation: New alert just arrived
- Click to view alert dropdown

ALERT TYPES:
- Supply-Demand Gap: Significant imbalance detected
- Velocity Anomaly: Unusual session creation rate
- Churn Risk: High-value customer at risk
- System Issue: Technical problem detected
- Revenue Threshold: Revenue targets not met

5.2 MANAGING ALERTS
-------------------
ACTIONS:
- View: Click alert to see full details
- Acknowledge: Mark as seen (stays in history)
- Dismiss: Remove from active alerts
- Snooze: Temporarily hide for 1h, 6h, or 24h

ALERT HISTORY:
- Access full history: Click "View History" in alert dropdown
- Filter by type, severity, date range
- Search specific alerts
- Export alert log for analysis

5.3 NOTIFICATION PREFERENCES
-----------------------------
Location: User Menu > Settings > Notifications

CHANNEL OPTIONS:
- In-app: Alerts in dashboard (always enabled)
- Email: Receive alert emails
- SMS: Text message for critical alerts (premium feature)
- Push: Browser push notifications

ALERT PREFERENCES:
- Choose which alerts to receive
- Set severity threshold (only high/critical)
- Quiet hours: Suppress non-critical alerts overnight
- Digest: Daily or weekly alert summary email

9. TROUBLESHOOTING
==================================================

9.1 COMMON ISSUES
-----------------

ISSUE: Data not loading
SOLUTION:
- Check internet connection
- Refresh browser (Cmd+R / Ctrl+R)
- Clear browser cache
- Try incognito/private window
- Contact support if persists

ISSUE: Charts not displaying
SOLUTION:
- Ensure JavaScript enabled in browser
- Update browser to latest version
- Disable browser extensions temporarily
- Try different browser (Chrome recommended)

ISSUE: Slow performance
SOLUTION:
- Close unused tabs
- Clear browser cache
- Check for large date ranges (reduce timeframe)
- Use Chrome or Safari for best performance

ISSUE: Can't export data
SOLUTION:
- Disable popup blockers
- Check download folder permissions
- Try different export format
- Reduce data range if export too large

ISSUE: Alerts not appearing
SOLUTION:
- Check notification settings
- Verify alert preferences enabled
- Check browser notification permissions
- Review alert history (may be dismissed)

9.2 BROWSER COMPATIBILITY
--------------------------
Supported Browsers:
- Chrome 90+ (recommended)
- Safari 14+
- Firefox 88+
- Edge 90+

Not Supported:
- Internet Explorer (any version)
- Old mobile browsers

10. FAQ
==================================================

Q: How often does data update?
A: Real-time metrics update every 30 seconds. Predictions refresh every 5 minutes.

Q: Can I customize my dashboard?
A: Yes! Drag-drop widgets, show/hide metrics, and save your layout.

Q: How do I invite team members?
A: Go to Settings > Team and click "Invite Member"

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. We use bank-level encryption and comply with SOC 2, GDPR, and HIPAA.

Q: Can I access historical data?
A: Yes, all data retained for 2 years. Enterprise plans: unlimited retention.

Q: What's the difference between forecast and prediction?
A: Both terms used interchangeably. We predict future supply/demand/churn.

Q: How accurate are predictions?
A: Typically 85-90% accurate. Confidence intervals show uncertainty range.

Q: Can I integrate with other tools?
A: Yes! We have APIs and integrations with Salesforce, Slack, and more.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Data retained for 30 days.

Q: Do you offer training?
A: Yes! Free onboarding training for all customers. Enterprise: dedicated training.

GLOSSARY
==================================================

Churn: When a customer stops using the service
Health Score: Composite metric (0-100) indicating customer/marketplace health
Velocity: Rate of session creation (sessions per hour)
Anomaly: Unusual data point outside expected range
Confidence Interval: Range where true value likely falls
ROI: Return on Investment (revenue divided by cost)
Cohort: Group of customers with common characteristics
Conversion: Desired action taken (signup, booking, etc.)
Engagement: Measure of how actively users interact
Retention: Percentage of customers who stay over time
